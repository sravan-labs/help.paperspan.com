# Saving Articles on iOS

To save an article from Safari or any browser:

  1. Tap the Share icon.

  2. Scroll and tap “PaperSpan”. If it's your first time, tap “More…” and enable it.

  3. Your article will be saved instantly.

Alternatively:
Copy any link to your clipboard and open the PaperSpan app.
You’ll see a prompt to save the copied link to your reading list — just tap Save, and it’s done.
