## Import into PaperSpan

You can import your existing saved articles from:

### Pocket
- Get your reading list from Pocket as a **CSV file**
- Upload the CSV from your [PaperSpan web](https://web.paperspan.com/settings?tab=import) dashboard
- The system will process and add your articles

>  **Note:** Importing may take up to **15 minutes**, depending on the file size and number of articles.

### Instapaper
- Export your reading list from Instapaper in **CSV format**
- Upload the CSV file via the [PaperSpan web](https://web.paperspan.com/settings?tab=import)  dashboard

---

## Export from PaperSpan

You can export your saved articles from PaperSpan to an **JSON file** at any time from the [PaperSpan web](https://web.paperspan.com/settings?tab=export)  dashboard.

- Includes your saved links and titles, highlights and other metadata in a clean format
