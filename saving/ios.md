# Saving Articles on iOS

1. In Safari or any browser app, tap the Share icon.
2. Scroll and tap “PaperSpan”. If first time, tap “More…” to enable it.
3. Your article will be saved instantly.

You can also copy a link to clipboard and open the app. You will find a prompt to save the copied link to your reading list. If you accept, the link will be saved.
