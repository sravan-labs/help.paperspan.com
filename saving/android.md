# Saving Articles on Android

To save an article from Chrome or any browser on Android:

  1. Tap the Menu (three dots) icon.

  2. Select Share.

  3. Tap “PaperSpan” from the list.
    If you don’t see it, tap “More” to enable it.

Your article will be saved instantly to your reading list.
